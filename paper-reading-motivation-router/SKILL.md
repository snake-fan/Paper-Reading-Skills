# Paper Reading Motivation Router

Use this skill before starting a paper-reading session.

Goal: identify why the user is reading now, then route the session to one of the motivation-specific skills.

## Routing Questions

Ask briefly:

1. Are you trying to understand a field, define a research problem, design a method, design experiments, support writing, or challenge your own idea?
2. What concrete output do you want after this reading session?
3. Are you reading one paper deeply, scanning many papers, or comparing a small set of close works?
